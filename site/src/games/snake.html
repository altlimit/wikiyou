How to Play

  • Press Start Game or any arrow key to begin.
  • Use Arrow Keys or WASD to steer the snake.
  • Eat the red food pellets to grow longer and earn points.
  • Avoid hitting the walls or your own tail — that ends the game!
  • Press Space to pause and resume.

About Snake

Snake is one of the most iconic video games of all time, with roots going back to the 1976 arcade game Blockade. It became a worldwide phenomenon when it was pre-loaded on Nokia mobile phones starting in 1998. The simple yet addictive gameplay — guiding a growing snake to eat food without colliding with itself — has made it a beloved classic across generations.
