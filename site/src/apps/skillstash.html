Overview

SkillStash is a web application designed specifically for tradespeople — contractors, electricians, plumbers, carpenters, and other skilled workers — who need a professional way to showcase their work. The platform allows users to create organized, shareable portfolios of their completed projects, replacing the common practice of scrolling through camera rolls or sending unorganized photo dumps to potential clients.

The service addresses a widespread problem in the trades industry: skilled workers often produce excellent work but struggle to present it professionally when bidding on new jobs. SkillStash provides a structured, polished way to document and display project history.

How It Works

SkillStash follows a three-step workflow designed for speed and simplicity:

  • Upload — Users upload site photos, contracts, permits, and other project documentation directly into the platform. The system handles organization and sorting.
  • AI-Powered Descriptions — The platform uses artificial intelligence to scan uploaded images and documents, automatically generating project descriptions, extracting dates, and tagging relevant skills used in each project.
  • Share — Each user receives a clean, professional portfolio URL (e.g., skillstash.net/your-name) that can be shared with potential clients, included in bids, or linked from business cards.

Features

  • Custom Professional URL — Each user gets a personalized portfolio link at skillstash.net/your-name.
  • Unlimited Projects — No cap on the number of projects or photos that can be documented.
  • Verified License Badges — Tradespeople can verify their professional licenses, which are displayed as trust badges on their portfolio.
  • AI Project Builder — Automatically generates project descriptions and metadata from uploaded photos and documents.
  • Mobile-Friendly — Designed for use on job sites with mobile devices.

Target Audience

SkillStash targets independent contractors and small trade businesses who need a professional online presence but don't have the time or technical skills to build and maintain a traditional website. The platform is particularly relevant for tradespeople bidding on high-value residential and commercial contracts where presentation quality can influence hiring decisions.

Pricing

SkillStash offers a free tier to get started with no credit card required. Premium plans with additional features are available through their pricing page.
